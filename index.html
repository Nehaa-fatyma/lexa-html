Autonomous Legal Reasoning Engine

Unlike generic AI tools, Lexa is trained on verified legal data and built to understand multi-step Legal logic - enabling it to draft, interpret, and suggest clauses with contentual intelligence, not just keywords

Instant Drafting of Complex Legal Documents

From contracts and legal notices to court submissions, Lexa drafts documents within seconds based on your input — with jurisdiction-specific clauses, formatting, and legal tone pre-set.

Collaboration with Clients and Colleagues

Invite clients, interns, or partners to review, comment, or edit documents — with role-based access and a full version history. Lexa helps streamline nternal workflows and client communication.
